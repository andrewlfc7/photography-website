Starry constellation Pt 2
Here on this night, where the vastness of the sky
Is filled with nothing but a constellation of stars
A sight to be awestruck by
Oh, I wish I could stay in this moment forever
I’m confused about whether this is a dream or reality
Just lying here, gazing at the beauty of the stars
Holding you in my arms
While you listen to the melody my heart plays in your presence
Hearing the waves hitting the shores
The concept of time ceases to exist
And in this moment, where I feel nothing but serenity and joy

Just to see you shining in the moonlight

Like a shooting star

Laugh Tale
Oh, I wish that, in
The moments when it's my time
To depart this life,
In the fleeting seconds
During the moment when
My life flashes before my eyes,
It flashes like a shooting star,
Reaching its climax.
Oh, I wish in that moment
To experience all the pain, sorrow, and joy
Of my life,
Of the various journeys,
And most importantly,
Of the chaos.
And at the moment just
Before it ends,
As my life slowly fades
And the shooting star reaches
The end of its journey,
Just before it slowly vanishes
Into the cosmos,
Leaving a sight to behold,
I wish to be laughing at it all,
Laughing at the insanity of being born,
Of the life I lived,
Of the good times,
Of the bad times,
And just at the end,
Laugh.
Oh, I wish.
